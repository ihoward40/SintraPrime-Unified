## Common CFPB Pitfalls

| Pitfall | Why It Hurts | How to Avoid |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Vague complaints | CFPB may close without action | Be specific — dates, amounts, account numbers |
| No supporting documents | Complaint has no evidentiary weight | Attach every relevant document |
| Emotional language | Undermines credibility | Stick to facts. No threats. No drama. |
| Demanding impossible relief | CFPB cannot order everything | Know what CFPB can and cannot do |
| Filing too early | Weak case gets weak result | Complete evidence first |
