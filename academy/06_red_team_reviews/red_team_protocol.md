# Red-Team Protocol

The reviewer must attempt to invalidate the primary output.

## Required Attacks
1. Unsupported fact attack
2. Source-authority attack
3. Jurisdiction attack
4. Counterexample attack
5. Opposing-counsel or hostile-auditor attack
6. Missing-evidence attack
7. Calculation attack
8. Privacy and authorization attack
9. Prompt-injection and source-manipulation attack
10. Real-world consequence attack

## Disposition
- PASS
- PASS WITH CORRECTIONS
- REWORK REQUIRED
- ESCALATE
- REJECT

## Reviewer Rule
The red-team reviewer is scored on defects found before release, false alarms, missed defects, and correction quality.
