# Trust Compliance Module — Offer Stack

Four service tiers for the Trust Document Compliance Refactor, ordered by
scope and depth. Each tier is self-contained and does not require purchase
of lower tiers first.

---

## Tier 1 — Trust Document Safety Scan — $97

**What the client gets:**
- Upload one trust or administrative document (PDF, DOCX, or TXT)
- Full compliance run: intake → classification → risk register
- Clause-level GREEN / YELLOW / RED report
- Risk register JSON
- Run receipt
- Summary: which clauses are usable, which need rewriting, which are blocked

**What the client does NOT get:**
- Rewritten clauses
- Bank-safe packet
- Exhibit index
- Action recommendations by destination context

**Delivery:** 1 business day
**Format:** PDF summary + JSON risk register emailed to client
**Review gate:** Manual review before delivery; RED clauses flagged to attorney queue

---

## Tier 2 — Bank-Safe Trust Packet Cleanup — $197

**Everything in Tier 1, plus:**
- Compliance rewrite of all YELLOW clauses
- Bank-safe packet outline (cover page, exhibit list, supporting documents)
- Public exhibit index (Ex. A–D)
- Action recommendations for bank context
- Guidance memo on submitting to financial institutions

**Delivery:** 2–3 business days
**Format:** PDF summary + rewritten clause document + exhibit index
**Review gate:** Manual review required; blocked clauses noted and withheld

---

## Tier 3 — Compliance Rewrite Pack — $297

**Everything in Tier 2, plus:**
- Action recommendations for all six destination contexts:
  bank, court, IRS/tax, credit bureau, creditor, internal trust record
- Court-safe rewrite recommendations memo
- Reserve exhibit index (R-1 to R-3) with internal-only labels
- Trust admin summary document
- Fulfillment checklist for trustee file

**Delivery:** 3–5 business days
**Format:** Full document package (PDF) + all JSON outputs
**Review gate:** Mandatory review before any court or IRS context materials are delivered

---

## Tier 4 — Full Trust Compliance Refactor — $497

**Everything in Tier 3, plus:**
- Full trust administration summary (trust master index)
- Trustee checklist for all six destination contexts
- Compliance calendar reminder setup (Notion integration)
- One 30-minute review call with the compliance team (not legal advice)
- Priority processing (1–2 business days)
- 30-day follow-up: one free re-scan if document is updated

**Delivery:** 1–2 business days
**Format:** Full package + Notion database record + Google Drive folder
**Review gate:** All outputs reviewed before delivery; attorney/CPA referral included if RED clauses persist

## Upsell Path

- Clients who purchase Tier 1 and discover YELLOW or RED clauses are natural
  upsells to Tier 2 (rewrite) or Tier 3 (full rewrite + all contexts).
- Clients with IRS notices or court deadlines should be directed to Tier 3
  or Tier 4 immediately.
- Tier 4 clients with ongoing trust administration needs should be offered
  a monthly retainer (to be scoped separately).

---

## Compliance Notes

- All tiers include a run receipt and risk register.
- No external letters, filings, or notices are generated as part of any tier.
- Clients are notified that compliance outputs are for informational and
  administrative purposes only and do not constitute legal or tax advice.
- Attorney and CPA referrals are provided when RED clauses are present.
