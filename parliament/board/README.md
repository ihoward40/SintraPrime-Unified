# SintraPrime Parliament Board

The Board is the executive governance layer for SintraPrime-Unified.

## Purpose

- Review major system decisions.
- Approve high-impact automation.
- Resolve cross-agent conflicts.
- Maintain accountability and audit records.
- Protect user authority, privacy, finances, and legal interests.

## Core Files

- `BOARD_CHARTER.md` — authority, scope, and operating rules.
- `MEMBERS.md` — board roles and responsibilities.
- `DECISION_LOG.md` — permanent decision record.
- `MEETING_AGENDA.md` — reusable meeting structure.

## Operating Principle

AI agents may research, draft, test, organize, and recommend. Final authority remains with Isiah Tarik Howard unless expressly delegated in writing.
