# Media Monetization Map

**Purpose:** Map the content ecosystem — every piece of content should drive toward a specific outcome.

**Core Rule:** No content should go out without knowing the next door it opens.

---

## The Funnel

```
Social Content (free, broad reach)
    ↓
Lead Magnets (free, opt-in required)
    ↓
Evidence Audit ($97)
    ↓
Blueprint Review ($397)
    ↓
Evidence Vault Support ($29/mo)
    ↓
Courses / Workshops / Templates (scalable)
```
