# Module 3 — Issue Spotting

## Required Categories
- Jurisdiction
- Standing
- Parties and capacity
- Deadlines and limitations
- Notice and service
- Claims and elements
- Defenses
- Burdens and standards
- Evidence
- Remedies
- Damages
- Preservation
- Appeal or review
- Ethical and authorization boundaries

## Method
For every issue, state:
1. Rule
2. Required elements
3. Supporting facts
4. Missing facts
5. Contrary facts
6. Risk
7. Recommended next step
