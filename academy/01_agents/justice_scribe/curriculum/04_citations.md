# Module 4 — Citation Integrity

## Citation Requirements
Every material legal proposition must be traceable to the actual source.

Each citation record must include:
- Source title
- Court or issuing body
- Jurisdiction
- Date
- Pinpoint page, paragraph, section, or line
- Source URL or repository path
- Date verified
- Authority classification

## Automatic Failure
- Fabricated case
- Fabricated quotation
- False pinpoint
- Citation to a source that does not support the proposition
