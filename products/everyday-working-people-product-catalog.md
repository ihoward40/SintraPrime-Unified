# IKE SOLUTIONS Everyday Working People Product Catalog

## Purpose
This catalog turns SintraPrime Unified into a practical product engine for everyday working people: employees, parents, drivers, renters, creators, gig workers, church members, caregivers, students, and small business starters.

The rule: sell recognizable relief at reasonable prices. Do not lead with complicated legal, trust, or automation language. Lead with outcomes people already understand.

Brand frame: IKE SOLUTIONS — practical AI tools, records organization, document help, family legacy, credit clarity, and everyday business support.
