## Key Correspondence Rules

1. **Log within 24 hours** of sending or receiving any communication.
2. **Save copies** of all sent correspondence to the matter's `correspondence/` folder.
3. **Include exhibits** — if a letter references exhibits, note them in the Summary column.
4. **Certified mail** — if sent by certified mail, note the tracking number in the Summary column.
5. **Phone calls** — log date, time, who spoke, and key points discussed. Do not rely on memory.
