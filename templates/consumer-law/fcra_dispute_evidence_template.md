# FCRA Dispute — Evidence Template

**Purpose:** Court-safe FCRA dispute template that requires evidence before allegations. Do not use this template until the evidence intake checklist is complete and the minimum readiness standard is met.

---

## ⚠️ RULE: Evidence Before Allegations

**Do not fill in the "Allegation" column until you have a source document in the "Evidence" column.**

A dispute letter without evidence is a paper airplane into a corporate brick wall.

---

## Dispute Preparation Checklist

| # | Item | Status |
|---|------|--------|
| 1 | Full credit report obtained (all 3 bureaus) | ⚪ |
| 2 | Trade line details captured (account #, balance, status, date opened, date closed) | ⚪ |
| 3 | Specific inaccuracy identified (wrong amount, wrong status, wrong date, not mine, etc.) | ⚪ |
| 4 | Supporting evidence located and cataloged | ⚪ |
| 5 | Prior dispute history checked | ⚪ |
| 6 | Furnisher identity confirmed | ⚪ |

**Ready to draft?** Only when ALL items above are checked.

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|

| Bureau conducts superficial reinvestigation | Cite specific inaccuracies, not general disputes |
| Dispute marked "frivolous" | Ensure each dispute item is specific and supported |
