## Keyword Scoring Formula

When ranking content ideas, apply this weighted score (0–10 per factor):

```
SCORE = (Purchase Intent × 0.50) + (Search Volume × 0.30) + (Low Competition Bonus × 0.20)
```

**Purchase Intent (0–10):** Does the searcher have a credit card in hand?
- 10 = "shopify free trial", "best shopify plan", "how to start a shopify store today"
- 7  = "shopify tutorial 2025", "how to sell digital products"
- 4  = "what is shopify", "ecommerce basics"
- 1  = "what is affiliate marketing" (informational, no urgency)

**Search Volume (0–10):** Relative monthly search volume
- 10 = 100K+/month
- 7  = 10K–100K/month
- 4  = 1K–10K/month
- 1  = <1K/month

**Low Competition Bonus (0–10):** Ability to rank or go viral
- 10 = Niche angle, low competition, unique perspective available
- 5  = Moderate competition, differentiation possible
- 0  = Saturated, dominated by major brands
