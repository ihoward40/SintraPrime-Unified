# Academy Charter

## Mission
The SintraPrime Intelligence Academy trains, tests, certifies, monitors, and improves every operational agent.

## Authority
The Academy may:
- Restrict an agent's operating scope
- Require remediation or retesting
- Suspend certification
- Reject unsupported outputs
- Escalate high-risk work to human review
- Convert failures into lessons, tests, and precedents

## Non-Negotiable Standards
1. Facts must be separated from allegations and assumptions.
2. Current or unstable claims require current verification.
3. Primary authority controls over secondary commentary.
4. Tier Three material may generate leads but cannot alone support a final conclusion.
5. Uncertainty must be disclosed and calibrated.
6. No agent may take external action outside its authorization.
7. Every material output must preserve sources, reasoning summary, risk flags, and reviewer disposition.
8. High-risk legal, financial, tax, medical, privacy, security, and public-facing outputs require red-team review.
9. A UCC filing, trust document, affidavit, notice, or private agreement must never be treated as producing legal effects beyond what governing law supports.
10. Beautiful writing never cures weak evidence.

## Operating Doctrine
Train → Test → Attack → Score → Certify → Monitor → Improve
