# TikTok Script 001 — "Why Basic Dispute Letters Fail"

**Product:** Consumer Evidence Command Center
**Duration:** 45-60 seconds
**Tone:** Firm, clear, street-smart, professional
**Target Audience:** People who have tried credit disputes and got nowhere

---

## Script

**[0:00-0:05] — Hook**

"You wrote the dispute letter. You mailed it certified. And nothing happened."

**[0:05-0:15] — The Problem**

"Here's why: Credit bureaus get millions of dispute letters every year. Yours is one of thousands they'll see today. Without evidence attached, it's just words on paper. And words on paper don't move anything."

**[0:15-0:30] — The Real Issue**

"Most people think the problem is the letter. It's not. The problem is the evidence file. If you don't have your contract, your payment history, your notices — you're sending a paper airplane into a brick wall."

**[0:30-0:45] — The Solution**

"Before you write another letter, ask yourself three questions:
One — What exactly is wrong?
Two — What proof do I have?
Three — What should I do next?

If you can't answer all three with documents in hand, you're not ready to dispute."

**[0:45-0:55] — Call to Action**

"We built the Consumer Evidence Command Center to help you answer those three questions. It's not credit repair. It's evidence organization. Link in bio."

**[0:55-0:60] — Close**

"Evidence first. Always."
