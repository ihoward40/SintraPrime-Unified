HOWARD RECOVERY INTAKE TEMPLATE GUIDE

1. Open any .json template in this folder.
2. Fill in the blanks without changing the field names.
3. Save a completed copy into the intake folder.
4. Howard Intake Watch Agent will process it automatically.
5. Good files move to processed. Bad files move to errors.
6. External action remains locked. These templates do not send, file, email, call, or contact anyone.

Minimum required fields:
- case_name or case_id
- evidence_type
- title
- source

Use case_name when possible. The Case ID Resolver will find the active case ID.
