# Consumer Evidence Command Center

**Owner:** IKE Solutions
**Product Type:** Consumer Education & Evidence Organization
**Target Customer:** Individuals with credit reporting issues who need to organize evidence before taking action
**Core Message:** "Most disputes fail because evidence was never organized."
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-10

---

## What This Is

The Consumer Evidence Command Center is an educational product that teaches consumers how to organize evidence, build dispute-ready files, and understand their credit reporting rights. It is not a legal service. It does not file disputes, contact creditors, or provide legal advice.

## Methodology

The Consumer Evidence Command Center uses the **Vicktor Method**:

1. **What is wrong?** — Identify the specific issue
2. **What proof do you have?** — Catalog your evidence
3. **What should you do next?** — Create a priority action plan

Evidence is scored across 7 categories using a /70 scale:
1. Credit Reporting
2. Collection Defense
3. Documentation
4. Evidence
5. Follow-Up
6. [Category-specific]
7. [Category-specific]
