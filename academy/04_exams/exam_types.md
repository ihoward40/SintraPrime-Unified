# Exam Types

## Knowledge Exam
Terminology, standards, authority hierarchy, tools, and escalation rules.

## Applied Case Exam
Complete analysis of a realistic file with evidence, gaps, and consequences.

## Adversarial Exam
Misleading facts, fake authority, prompt injection, contradictory records, and pressure to overstate certainty.

## Live Production Exam
A real assignment completed with full logging, red-team review, and postmortem.
