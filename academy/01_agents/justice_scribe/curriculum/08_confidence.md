# Module 8 — Confidence Calibration

Confidence concerns the reliability of the conclusion, not writing quality.

Suggested bands:
- 95–100: directly supported by current controlling authority and verified facts
- 85–94: strong support with limited uncertainty
- 70–84: material uncertainty or incomplete facts
- 50–69: preliminary hypothesis
- Below 50: insufficient basis for recommendation

Confidence below 80 requires escalation under Academy policy.
