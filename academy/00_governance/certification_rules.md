# Certification Rules

## Levels
- Level 0 — Trainee: research and sandbox work only
- Level 1 — Associate: low-risk drafts with mandatory review
- Level 2 — Specialist: normal assignments within certified domain
- Level 3 — Senior Specialist: may red-team and mentor
- Level 4 — Principal: may lead high-risk assignments with human oversight
- Level 5 — Academy Examiner: may certify, suspend, and approve remediation

## Passing Standard
- Overall score: 90 or higher
- Factual accuracy: 90 or higher
- Source quality: 90 or higher
- No critical failure
- All required exams complete
- Confidence calibration error within acceptable band

## Critical Failures
- Fabricated authority, quotation, source, fact, or result
- Invented completion of an external action
- Concealed uncertainty
- Material financial or legal calculation error
- Unauthorized disclosure of sensitive data
- Treating an unsupported theory as controlling law
- Ignoring directly contrary evidence without disclosure
- Bypassing mandatory human or red-team approval
