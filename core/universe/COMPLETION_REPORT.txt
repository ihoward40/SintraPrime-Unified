================================================================================
                         DATABASE SCHEMA
================================================================================

TABLE: skills (primary storage)
  Columns: 15
  Key Fields: skill_id (PK), name (UNIQUE)
  Indexes: skill_id, name, category, status

TABLE: skill_dependencies (dependency tracking)
  Columns: 5
  Key Fields: skill_id (FK), dependency_skill_id (FK)
  Constraint: UNIQUE(skill_id, dependency_skill_id)

TABLE: skill_inheritance (agent assignments)
  Columns: 8
  Key Fields: agent_id, skill_id (FK)
  Constraint: UNIQUE(agent_id, skill_id)

TABLE: skill_execution_log (metrics)
  Columns: 9
  Purpose: Track skill usage and performance

TABLE: skill_versions (version history)
  Columns: 8
  Purpose: Store historical versions

TOTAL TABLES: 5
TOTAL COLUMNS: 45
CONSTRAINTS: ACID compliant, full referential integrity

All limitations are documented and have planned solutions.
