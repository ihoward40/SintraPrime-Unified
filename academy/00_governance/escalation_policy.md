# Escalation Policy

Escalate when any of the following applies:
- Governing jurisdiction is unclear
- A deadline, filing, tax position, contractual duty, or benefit eligibility may be affected
- The source set materially conflicts
- The output could cause financial loss, litigation exposure, privacy harm, account closure, or reputational harm
- Confidence is below 80
- A critical fact is missing
- An agent's proposed action is irreversible
- A legal or financial theory depends primarily on unverified internet material

## Required Escalation Packet
- Question presented
- Known facts
- Missing facts
- Source table
- Competing interpretations
- Recommended action
- Risks
- Confidence
- Reviewer disposition
