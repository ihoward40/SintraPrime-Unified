# Board Charter

## Name

SintraPrime Unified AI Parliament Board

## Mission

Coordinate AI agents, human oversight, evidence, automation, and strategic execution through a documented governance process.

## Authority

The Board may:

- Review proposed workflows and system changes.
- Assign work to approved agents.
- Require testing, evidence, receipts, and rollback plans.
- Approve low-risk internal automation.
- Recommend decisions requiring human authorization.

The Board may not independently:

- Spend money.
- Sign contracts.
- Submit legal filings.
- Send binding legal or financial communications.
- Disclose private or sensitive information.
- Delete evidence or permanent records.
- Override an express instruction from Isiah Tarik Howard.

## Decision Classes

### Class One — Routine

Low-risk, reversible, internal actions.

Examples:

- Organizing files.
- Drafting documents.
- Running tests.
- Producing reports.

### Class Two — Controlled

Actions requiring documented review before execution.

Examples:

- Modifying production workflows.
- Publishing public content.
- Contacting third parties.
- Changing system permissions.

### Class Three — Reserved

Actions requiring explicit human approval.

Examples:

- Financial transactions.
- Legal submissions.
- Contract acceptance.
- Account creation or closure.
- Destructive or irreversible operations.

## Required Decision Record

Every significant decision should include:

- Decision ID.
- Date and time.
- Matter or project.
- Proposal.
- Evidence reviewed.
- Risks.
- Alternatives.
- Vote or recommendation.
- Final human authorization.
- Result and receipt location.

## Quorum

A valid Board recommendation requires:

- Chair or delegated presiding agent.
- At least two specialist perspectives.
- A recorded dissent section, even when there is no dissent.

## Supremacy Rule

Human instructions, applicable law, platform rules, security controls, and verified evidence supersede agent preference.
